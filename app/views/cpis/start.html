About this prototype

This prototype explores how we could introduce Child Protection Information Sharing content to the redesigned SCRa.

A child care alert will be added to SCRa to inform health workers, with the relevant access, of any Child Protection Order (CPO) in place and information about the local authority that added it. If a health worker views the information, the relevant local authority and social workers will be informed of who viewed the CPO and their organisation.
